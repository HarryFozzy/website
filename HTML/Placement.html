Week 1 (4.7.22 - 8.7.22)

I had a very action packed first week. I learnt all about DriveWorks the company. I had introductions to every team that we have, from the Develoment Team to the Sales Team. As well as all of the introductions I also had problem solving training and learnt about the approach to work at DriveWorks. I learnt more about a company in one week than I have anything else.

Week 2 (11.7.22 - 15.7.22)

This week was much more DriveWorks 'The Software' based. I learnt DriveWorksExpress and DriveWorksSolo in this week, ending up with 2 new certifications at the end. DriveWorksExpress and DriveWorksSolo certifications.

Week 3 (18.7.22 - 22.7.22)

This week focused on the final section of my training in the solfware. The DriveWorks Pro software. I was the first to complete my Pro training and get my DriveWorks Pro certification. This week I was also given an inroduction to the company by the CEO and the VP.

Week 4 (25.7.22 -29.7.22)

To further my knowledge in DriveWorks this week I started my first DriveWorks Pro project. My manager tasked me with recreating the Scissor Lift demo that is on the DriveWorks website. This was a challenging task as I had only had the basic training but I learnt lots very quickly this way. This week there was also an onboarding activity for all of the new employees which was a great time to solcialise and meet some great people. Ending the week with a project review with my manager who was very impressed with the progress the interns were making.

DriveWorks Ltd

From the 4th of July 2022 until the 3rd of July 2023 I worked at DriveWorks Ltd. They are a Software company based on the outskirts of Warrington. DriveWorks is a CAD Automation Software. I worked in the Technical Team, which involved QA, Support and Demo creation. I learnt so much from this placement and not just about software but how a business is ran internally.

Timeline

  1. Week 1An action packed first week, learning about DriveWorks Ltd. With introductions to every team, from Develoment to the Team. As well as problem solving training.
  2. Week 2 This week was much more DriveWorks 'The Software' based. I learnt DriveWorksXpress and DriveWorksSolo in this week, ending up with 2 new certifications at the end. DriveWorksExpress and DriveWorksSolo certifications.
  3. Week 3 This week focused on the final section of my training in the solfware. The DriveWorks Pro software. I was the first to complete my Pro training and get my DriveWorks Pro certification. This week I was also given an inroduction to the company by the CEO and the VP.
  4. Week 4 To further my knowledge I started my first DriveWorks Pro Project. I was tasked with recreating the Scissor Lift Demo from the DriveWorksLive website. This was a challenging task as having had only the basic training but I learnt very quickly. This week also included an onboarding activity for all of the interns which was a great time to solcialise and meet everyone.
  5. Week 5 Starting the week with a Project review with my manager. He was very impressed with my progress.
